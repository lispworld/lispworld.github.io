The World of Lisp

The vastness that is Lisp -- throughout its more than half-century of existence -- can be a bwildering realm for the newcomer. The goal of this site is to bring some order to the perceived chaos:

We can get you started quicly by helping you find the Lisp that's right for you, showing you what it looks like and how it relates to other Lisps, and ultimately, accessing resources to learn more.
